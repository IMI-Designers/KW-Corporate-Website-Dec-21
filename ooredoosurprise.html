FAQ

What is Ooredoo Surprise?

Ooredoo Surprise gives you amazing offers, tailored exclusively to your needs. You can get internet, voice calls, international minutes, extra validity and even buy Nojoom points with generous discounts!

How can I access my Ooredoo Surprise?

Go to Ooredoo Surprise on My Ooredoo App or login via your web account: www.ooredoo.com.kw/myaccount. You can also send ‘S’ to 400 through SMS.

Are the offers always the same each time?

No, Ooredoo Surprise offers are always changing! Keep up to date on My Ooredoo App to check for new Surprise offers.

Are the offers given on Ooredoo Surprise permanent?

No, Ooredoo Surprise offers will be shown for a short time, so if you like an offer, hurry and subscribe before it is too late!

What are Ooredoo Surprise badges?

Ooredoo Surprise customers will win a badge for every Ooredoo Surprise subscription. When you collect 5 badges, the next Surprise offer of your choice will be free!

How can I check my usage of the offers I have taken from Ooredoo Surprise?

Once you subscribe to a Surprise offer, you can follow your usage and balance on the My Ooredoo App home page or through your web account: www.ooredoo.com.kw/myaccount.

I took a certain offer from Ooredoo Surprise, how can I take it again?

Ooredoo Surprise offers are not renewed automatically, so you will need to keep checking Ooredoo Surprise on the app or web to see if you can receive the same offer again or an even better offer!
